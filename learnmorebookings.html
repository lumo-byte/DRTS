Yard Test

The yard test evaluates your ability to control a vehicle in a closed environment. This includes tasks like reversing, parking, and completing a three-point turn.

Pass Mark:70 Points out of 100

Tip: Practice your maneuvering skills at a driving school before attempting this test.

Step 1: Parallel Parking

Align your car properly for parallel parking. Use mirrors and signals correctly.

Step 2: Three-Point Turn

Execute a safe three-point turn within the marked area, avoiding lines.

Step 3: Reversing

Reverse your car in a straight line while maintaining full control.

Step 4: Parking in a Bay

Enter and exit a parking bay without hitting the cones.

Step 5: Emergency Stop

Practice stopping quickly and safely as per examinerâ€™s instructions.

Road Test

The road test assesses your driving skills in real traffic conditions. You will be evaluated on lane discipline, signaling, and overall road safety.

Pass Mark:70 points out of 100

Tip: Stay calm and follow all traffic rules carefully during the test.

Step 1: Urban Driving

Navigate city streets while observing speed limits and traffic signals.

Step 2: Highway Driving

Maintain safe distances and lane discipline on highways.

Step 3: Emergency Situations

Respond calmly to sudden stops or obstacles as instructed by the examiner.
